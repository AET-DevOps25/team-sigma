2. To check the status of your pods:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.global.namespace }}

3. To view the logs of a specific service:
   kubectl logs -f deployment/<service-name> -n {{ .Values.global.namespace }}

4. To port-forward to a service locally:
   kubectl port-forward svc/<service-name> <local-port>:<service-port> -n {{ .Values.global.namespace }}
